What does Gameday Parking do?

Gameday Parking is a robust web-app meant to connect people looking for parking with people looking to lease.

Specifically, users will be able to navigate many parking availabilities and request/reserve parking spots through the app. Admins will be able to post available parking spots with details (amenities, size of space, grill availability, etc)

What we have done so far?

In the first iteration, we created necessary database models and create a basic web interface to populate thsoe models.

After the second iteration, parking spot owners are allowed to list their parking spots on the website and communicate with parking spot finders through Reviews, Ratings, and messaging system.

During the third iteration, we implemented in-application payment system Stripe, add features to the profile page, and allow users to upload pictures and get Email notifications.
